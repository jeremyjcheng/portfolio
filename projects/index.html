EcoNaut

Built a full-stack web application with React/TypeScript frontend and Flask backend, featuring ocean health monitoring dashboard with interactive maps and analytics. Recipe Rating Predictor

Used the provided Food.com dataset to explore, clean, and prepare training data with scikit-learn and pandas. Engineered features including one-hot tags, ingredient flags, rating statistics, contributor encoding, and nutrition ratios. Trained and tuned a RandomForestClassifier with GridSearchCV and evaluated results on holdout data
